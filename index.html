About SIP

We provide Insurance and wholesale services.

Opening for business in 2010, SIP Insurance Services (SIP) was created by industry professionals looking to improve on current wholesale methods. SIP has over 75 years of Insurance and wholesale knowledge in its management team. This experience has created a tremendous number of relationships, the foundation for the entire company. It is these connections that have facilitated the creation of SIP, while also helping to establish it as an industry leader in coverage placement and strategy. By simplifying the gap between the retail agents and the surplus lines carriers, SIP has further solidified the wholesale broker’s “value added” approach to assisting retail agents. The team approach is also evident in all aspects of business from application to binding. The goal is to provide a seamless process for retail agents allowing them the ability to service their clients in the best manner possible. This symbiotic process has been created from the ground up with our brokers in mind.

SIP Insurance Services is strategically located in heart of Orange County as a central point for all of Southern California. Our team is made of seasoned insurance professionals with one goal in mind, to bring the best surplus lines products and services to retail agents.
